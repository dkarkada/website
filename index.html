About Me

I'm a student at UT Austin (although I'm originally from the Houston area) studying physics, astronomy, and computer science. I'm still trying to figure out what I really like (in terms of career plans), although I'm currently leaning towards pursuing research.

Cont'd

I've recently been interested in the intersection of astrophysics and computational methods, nonlinear dynamics, machine perception (specifically audition), quantum computing, and STEM education. Of course, these are not entirely related to each other, so I'll have to narrow down my focus at some point!

Cont'd

In my free time I like to do light coding, hang out with friends, listen to (and make) electronic music (especially experimental stuff), work out and/or swim, browse timesink websites, and mull over the looming uncertainty of the future.
